COLLEGE MAPS

Navigating your campus made easy

About

College maps was made to make your life easier. As students we know how hard it can be to navigate a large and complicated campus, and it can be especially difficult for those visiting or adjusting to the locations of new classes. This tool was developed so that those who are unfamiliar with a campus can find a building and any classroom within that building. You'll be taken through the hallways of a building just like any other navigation app guides you through a maze of streets.

How to Use

To go to the mapping page, click the button below the logo. Once you're there, use the search bar to navigate either to a prelisted destination in the menu or to a building or room of your choice. If you need additional help or have questions about the software, use the contact us link above. Click about us to read more about the developers and our journey developing College Maps.
